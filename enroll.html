Enroll Badian NHS

Welcome to Badian National High School Enrollment Page! We are delighted that you have chosen to join our school community. We understand that selecting the right educational institution is a significant decision, and we are honored that you have entrusted us with your education.

At Badian NHS, we believe in providing a nurturing and inclusive environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to delivering a high-quality education that prepares students for success in their future endeavors. We are excited that you are considering enrolling at our school. Below, you will find all the information you need to begin the enrollment process.

Enrollment Process

  • Start by downloading our application form or filling it out online.
  • Complete all sections of the application form, providing accurate and up-to-date information.
  • Submit the completed application form along with any required documents, such as academic transcripts, recommendation letters, or a copy of your birth certificate.

Requirements

For Old Students

  • Form 9 or Report Card: Please provide a copy of your most recent Form 9 or Report Card, which showcases your academic progress and achievements.

We are excited to have you back as part of the Badian National High School community and look forward to another successful academic year together!

For Incoming Grade 11 Students (Not from Badian NHS Completers)

  • Form 9 or Report Card: Please submit a copy of your most recent Form 9 or Report Card, which helps us understand your academic background.
  • Certificate of Good Moral: Obtain a Certificate of Good Moral from your previous school, highlighting your positive character and behavior.
  • Photocopy of NSO/PSA: Provide a photocopy of your NSO/PSA (National Statistics Office/Philippine Statistics Authority) document, serving as proof of your identity and citizenship.

For Transferees and Incoming Grade 7 Students

  • Form 9 or Report Card: Please submit a copy of your most recent Form 9 or Report Card, which helps us understand your academic background and progress.
  • Certificate of Good Moral: Obtain a Certificate of Good Moral from your previous school, highlighting your positive character and behavior. This certificate assures us that you possess the values and conduct expected of a student at Badian National High School.
  • Photocopy of NSO/PSA: Provide a photocopy of your NSO/PSA (National Statistics Office/Philippine Statistics Authority) document. This serves as proof of your identity and citizenship, ensuring that we have accurate information on file.

These requirements are necessary for us to assess your eligibility for enrollment at Badian National High School. We value both your academic achievements and your character development. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our admissions office. We look forward to welcoming you as a new student and being a part of your educational journey at Badian National High School!
