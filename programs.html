Programs Badian NHS

Junior High School

At Badian National High School, we take pride in offering a diverse range of programs in our Junior High School to cater to the unique interests and talents of our students. Our programs are designed to provide a well-rounded education while nurturing their specific passions and abilities. Here are the programs we offer:

General Program

Our General program is designed to provide a well-balanced education that covers a wide range of subjects. Students in the General program will have the opportunity to explore various fields of study, including mathematics, sciences, languages, social sciences, and humanities. This program aims to provide a solid foundation for future academic pursuits and personal development.

Science, Technology, and Engineering (STE)

Our STE program is perfect for students who have a keen interest in the fields of science, technology, and engineering. Through hands-on experiments, research projects, and interactive learning experiences, students will develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills. They will also have access to state-of-the-art laboratories and cutting-edge technology to explore various scientific concepts.

Special Program in the Arts (SPA)

For students who have a flair for creativity and a deep passion for the arts, our SPA program provides a comprehensive curriculum in various artistic disciplines. From visual arts, music, dance, theater, to creative writing, students will have the opportunity to refine their skills and express their artistic talents. They will receive guidance from experienced faculty members and have access to well-equipped studios and performance spaces.

Regardless of the program chosen, all our students benefit from a supportive and inclusive learning environment, dedicated teachers, and a comprehensive curriculum that meets national educational standards. We believe in fostering a love for learning, promoting critical thinking, and preparing our students for success in their future endeavors.

Senior High School

At Badian National High School, we offer a comprehensive range of programs in our Senior High School to provide our students with a solid foundation for higher education or to prepare them for the workforce. Our programs are designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in their chosen paths. Here are the programs we offer:

Academic Track

Our Academic Track is designed for students who wish to pursue further studies in college or university. This track offers three strands that cater to different areas of interest:

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

This strand focuses on subjects related to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Students in this strand will develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills, preparing them for careers in fields such as engineering, medicine, computer science, and research.

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

The ABM strand prepares students for careers in business, finance, entrepreneurship, and management. Through a combination of business, accounting, economics, and other related subjects, students will develop skills in financial analysis, marketing, strategic planning, and business management.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

The HUMSS strand is ideal for students interested in pursuing careers in the fields of humanities, social sciences, communication, and the arts. Students will explore subjects such as literature, history, psychology, sociology, and political science, which will develop their critical thinking, communication, and research skills.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

Our TVL Track offers students the opportunity to gain practical skills and knowledge that are directly applicable to the workforce. This track consists of various strands, including:

Information and Communications Technology (ICT)

Programming: This strand focuses on computer programming and software development. Students will learn programming languages, software engineering principles, and develop skills in developing applications and systems. Computer Systems Servicing (CSS): This strand emphasizes the technical aspects of computer systems, including hardware troubleshooting, network installation and maintenance, and software configuration.

Arts and Design

This strand focuses on developing students' artistic skills and creativity in various art forms such as visual arts, multimedia, graphic design, and performing arts. Students will have the opportunity to express themselves artistically and explore different mediums of artistic expression.

Entrepreneurship and Innovation Management (EIM)

The EIM strand is designed to develop students' entrepreneurial mindset and skills. Students will learn about business planning, marketing, financial management, and innovation strategies. This strand prepares students for careers in entrepreneurship or for further studies in business-related fields.

Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW)

The SMAW strand provides students with hands-on training in welding techniques and safety procedures. Students will learn about different welding processes, equipment operation, and gain practical skills in metal fabrication and joining.

Cookery

The Cookery strand focuses on developing students' skills in culinary arts and food preparation. Students will learn about cooking techniques, food safety and sanitation, menu planning, and nutrition. This strand prepares students for careers in the culinary industry or for further studies in culinary arts.

Caregiving

The Caregiving strand in the HE strand emphasizes providing students with the knowledge and skills needed to care for individuals with special needs or those requiring assistance with daily activities. Students will learn about caregiving principles, basic medical procedures, communication skills, and ethical considerations. This strand prepares students for careers in caregiving, healthcare, or for further studies in nursing or related fields.

Regardless of the chosen track, all our Senior High School students benefit from a supportive and inclusive learning environment, dedicated teachers, and a comprehensive curriculum that meets national educational standards. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are academically competent, socially responsible, and equipped for success in their chosen paths.
