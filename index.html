RESEARCH CARAVAN & CONGRESS 2023

Badian NHS organized a research caravan and congress exclusively for Grade 10 STE (Science, Technology, and Engineering) students. The event aimed to provide an avenue for young researchers to showcase their scientific projects and foster a spirit of inquiry among the participants.

The exhibits encompassed a wide range of scientific disciplines, including biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science. Each project was meticulously presented through visually appealing posters, prototypes, and interactive displays, captivating the attention of both fellow students and esteemed judges.

Throughout the day, participants had the opportunity to engage in fruitful discussions with their peers, teachers, and judges, allowing them to gain valuable feedback and insights into their research projects. The judges carefully evaluated each exhibit based on the project's scientific merit, creativity, and presentation skills. The winners were announced during the closing ceremony, where they were commended for their exceptional efforts and dedication to scientific exploration.

The research caravan and congress served as a platform for budding scientists to showcase their innovative ideas and discoveries. It not only encouraged the students to delve deeper into scientific research but also fostered a sense of healthy competition and collaboration among them. The event undoubtedly left a lasting impact on the participants, inspiring them to pursue further scientific endeavors and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their respective fields.

About

About Us

Badian National High School (BNHS) is one of two high schools in Badian. This is also the larger of the two and many students come from neighboring towns to study here. Badian National High School located at the center of the town, nearby the marketplace Badian Public Market and the sports venue Badian Sports Complex.

At Badian National High School, we believe that education is not just about academics, but also about character formation and holistic development. Our faculty and staff are dedicated to nurturing our students into well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in life.
